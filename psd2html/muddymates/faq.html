Muddy Mates FAQ

  • 1

    What Should I Wear?

    You can come in custom, athletic wear, older clothes, or whatever you want to run with. You may not run barefoot. Shoes are required.

  • 2

    Do I have to run the entire course

    No. Some people are running to compete in their age division and others are just out to get muddy and have fun!.

  • 3

    Can I run with my friends/family?

    Yes. When you and your friends or family sign up, note on the registration who you want to run with, and we'll do our best to put you all in the same group..

  • 4

    What do I have to bring

    Other than your running clothes and trainers, bring a complete change of clothes and a towel. You will be covered in mud after the event! There will be outdoor showers.

  • 5

    What time should you arrive at the park?

    Please arrive at least 30 minutes prior to your wave starting time. Anyone registering after September 1st may or may not receive a tee shirt depending on availability.
