They say that necessity is the mother of invention.

Welcome

Working as full time racetrack veterinarians, we began to talk about the products that were missing from our day to day practice. We created Racing Vet to make those products a reality. At Racing Vet we ask questions before we begin the manufacturing process. Most importantly, does it work? Is it practical? Is it well priced? If we can answer those questions, we are going to bring that product to our customers. Very simple.

If you would like to purchase products through our website, please rest assured that we have a secure online payment process and your order will be delivered to your door.

Thank you for taking the time to visit the Racing Vet website. If you have any comments, suggestions, or simply have a question about one of our products, please don’t hesitate to contact us, because we are always happy to hear from our customers.

Dr Lara Tweedie
Director, Racing Vet Pty Ltd
